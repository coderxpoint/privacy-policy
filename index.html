Privacy Policy for SEO AnalyzerX

SEO AnalyzerX respects user privacy. This Chrome extension does not collect, store, or transmit personal data or personally identifiable information.

The extension only analyzes the content of the webpage currently selected by the user in order to provide SEO analysis and optimization insights. All processing is performed locally within the user’s browser.

The extension does not track users across websites, does not collect browsing history, and does not share data with third parties.
